Hi, I'm Winston. I am from Shanxi, a northern province of China, which is why I don't have an accent. I can help you to learn standard pronunciation of Mandarin, which is spoken by two thirds of Chinese people.
I have an ielts score of 7.0, which means we can understand each other and study without obstacles. And I obtained my master’s degree in Renewable energy in Newcastle University, UK. This experience let me gain a cross-cultural perspective and empathy, which is important in language learning and teaching.
I know the difficulties and traps well when we touch a new language, and the skills to learn Chinese easily and interestingly. And I have taught adults and exchange students Chinese both in reality and online.
I can teach students with different purposes: exam, daily conversation, business, travel, etc. There are textbooks, feedbacks, homework and formal tests for you to improve ability of Chinese. You can choose comprehensive learning (Listening/Speaking/Writing/Reading) or just one area.
Students over 6 are welcome! Just go for it!
